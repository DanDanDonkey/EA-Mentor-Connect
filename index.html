What happens after registration?

1. Review Period

We review submissions weekly and process matches based on shared interests and availability.

2. Match Notification

You will receive an email with your match details, including a brief profile and contact information.

3. Schedule Meeting

Use the provided scheduling link to find a time that works for both parties. We recommend LettuceMeet.
